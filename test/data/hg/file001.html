Avalanche problem #1 : Wind slabs

Signs of wind transport are factors to look for when traveling at treeline and in the alpine, especially on N slopes and near landforms where S winds formed wind slabs. These could be triggered by a skier and start a small avalanche that would take it to slide on the icy crust toward obstacles and cause serious injury.
