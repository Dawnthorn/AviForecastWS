Avalanche bulletin for the Chic-Chocs

Area concerned:Mont Albert, mont Hog’s Back, Champs-de-Mars, mont Lyall, mont Vallières-de-Saint-Réal, mont Blanche-Lamontagne et Mines-Madeleine.

Issued on : Friday 24 January 2014 à 7:30
Valid until : Sunday 26 January 2014 à 7:30

Danger ratings Friday Saturday Outlook Sunday
Alpine ModerateConsiderableConsiderable
Treeline ModerateConsiderableConsiderable
Below treeline LowModerateModerate

Avalanche problem #1 : Wind slabs

Strong S winds expected Saturday will cause significant snow transport that will create new wind slabs onto the January 14th crust near landforms of NW to NE slopes in the alpine and at treeline .

Avalanche problem #2 : Persistent slab

The weak layer of facets buried above the January 14th crust should be monitored at all altitudes because of the additional load caused by wind transport and new snow predicted for the next few days.

Avalanche problem #3 : Loose dry

The new snow expected for the weekend will increase the possibility of triggering small avalanches on steep slopes below treeline .
